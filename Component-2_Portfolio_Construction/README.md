## Project Overview

Following the evaluation of the existing portfolio, a new equity portfolio was constructed with the objective of achieving long-term capital appreciation while maintaining an appropriate risk-return balance.

A virtual investment budget of ₹10,00,000 was allocated across carefully selected companies from diverse sectors based on fundamental strength, growth potential, market leadership, and financial performance.

---

## Investment Objective

To construct a diversified equity portfolio that:

* Maximizes long-term returns
* Minimizes concentration risk
* Maintains sector diversification
* Invests in fundamentally strong businesses
* Balances growth and stability

---

## Stock Selection Framework

Extensive market research was conducted to identify high-quality companies across sectors.

The following parameters were evaluated and compared against industry benchmarks:

### Financial Strength

* Company Reserves
* Earnings Per Share (EPS)
* Book Value

### Valuation & Market Position

* Price-to-Earnings Ratio (P/E)
* Industry Leadership
* Competitive Advantage

### Market Performance

* 52-Week High and Low Analysis
* Historical Stock Performance
* Growth Trends

### Business Quality

* Revenue Growth
* Profitability
* Market Share
* Future Growth Potential
